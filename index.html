Tokenomics Overview

SilverBean (SBN) is an ERC-20 token on Polygon designed for tipping and social engagement via TipBot. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Total Supply: 100 million SBN
  • Initial Price: $0.00000707
  • Circulating Supply (Early): ~26.5 million SBN
  • Allocations:
    • Liquidity Pool (LP): 15% (15 million SBN + 1 billion Wrkz)
    • TipBot CEXswap: 1.5% (1.5 million SBN + 100 million Wrkz)
    • DODO LP Mining Rewards: 50% (50 million SBN, 27,379 SBN/day, ~120-150% APY)
    • DODO Single Staking Farm: 10% (10 million SBN, 5,476 SBN/day, ~60% APY)
    • Airdrops/Community: 10% (10 million SBN)
    • Team/Dev: 10% (10 million SBN, vested 2 years)
    • Reserve: 8.5% (8.5 million SBN)
  • Emissions: 30,118 SBN/day (LP Mining: 27,379, Single Staking: 5,476)
  • Drainage: ~61.14 million Wrkz/month, 50% LP Wrkz in ~8.2 months

Learn more about Wrkz mining and SBN utility on TipBot.

How to Stake in DODO LP Mining Pool

Earn up to ~120-150% APY by providing liquidity to the SBN/Wrkz LP and staking DLP tokens on DODO.

  1. Acquire SBN and Wrkz: Buy SBN on TipBot CEXswap or QuickSwap, and mine Wrkz (14.4 million/day emissions) via FastPool.
  2. Add Liquidity on DODO: Visit DODO, connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask), and add SBN + Wrkz to the SBN/Wrkz LP (15 million SBN + 1 billion Wrkz). Receive DLP tokens.
  3. Stake DLP Tokens: Go to the DODO farming page, select the SBN/Wrkz LP mining pool, and stake your DLP tokens.
  4. Claim Rewards: Earn 27,379 SBN/day proportional to your LP share (e.g., 1 million SBN = ~1,826 SBN/day). Claim rewards instantly and optionally stake them for more yield.
  5. Monitor: Check your APY and adjust liquidity as needed. Watch for impermanent loss mitigated by DODO’s PMM.

How to Stake in DODO Single Staking Pool

Earn ~60% APY by staking SBN in the DODO single staking farm.

  1. Acquire SBN: Purchase SBN from TipBot CEXswap, QuickSwap, or earn via LP mining.
  2. Stake on DODO: Visit DODO, connect your wallet, and navigate to the single staking farm. Stake your SBN (e.g., 1 million SBN = ~1,096 SBN/day).
  3. Claim Rewards: Earn 5,476 SBN/day proportional to your stake. Claim rewards instantly.
  4. Hold or Use: Use rewards for tipping on TipBot or hold for future value.
  5. Monitor: Track your APY and adjust stake as needed.
